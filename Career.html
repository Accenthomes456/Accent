Payroll Clerk

Entry-Level | Full-Time

Accent Home Remedies is seeking a reliable and detail-oriented Payroll Clerk to join our team. In this role, you will help ensure accurate and timely payroll processing for our staff.

No prior payroll experience is necessary — we provide full training. This is an excellent opportunity to begin or grow your career in administrative finance with a supportive and growing construction company.

`

Key Responsibilities

  • Assist with preparing and processing weekly and bi-weekly payroll
  • Maintain accurate records of employee hours and pay adjustments
  • Review timesheets and ensure data accuracy
  • Support payroll tax and deduction handling
  • Handle employee payroll questions respectfully and confidentially

Qualifications

  • High school diploma or equivalent
  • Strong attention to detail and accuracy
  • Basic computer skills (Excel experience a plus)
  • Good organizational and communication skills
  • Trustworthy with sensitive information

What We Offer

  • Paid training and mentorship
  • Growth opportunities in admin or HR roles
  • Competitive pay and a supportive work environment
  • Paid time off and potential benefits
